The Booker Prize Longlist has been announced and for many people that sets their reading goals for the next few months!

"There is an emphasis on family stories; sometimes intimate and direct (Claire Adam's Love Forms and Ben Markovitz's The Rest of Our Lives) …but more often as a lens through which to examine the forces of history (Tash Aw's The South and Susan Choi's Flashlight)."

The Guardian

The list takes readers around the world to explore different places and experiences. There are likely to be writers you haven't encountered before (two debut novels on the list), a few authors you are surprised not to see (Our Evenings by Alan Hollinghurst perhaps?) while fans of Kiran Desai's The Inheritance of Loss will be pleased to see her new epic The Loneliness of Sonia and Sunny on the list (we can't wait to read it!). Nine of the books on the list of 13 contenders are first time Booker Prize nominees.

We've made a stack of the Booker Prize Longlist for you to explore and you might also want to see a sample of the new titles out in August.

Enjoy the reading!
